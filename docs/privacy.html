Privacy Policy

LiftTracker · Last updated 6 July 2026

LiftTracker is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Data we collect

None. LiftTracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics.

Where your data lives

Every workout, weight, and setting you enter is stored only on your device. The app makes no network connections and sends nothing to us or any third party. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it.

Tracking

LiftTracker does not track you across apps or websites and contains no advertising or third-party SDKs.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Because no data is collected, no information from children is gathered.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
